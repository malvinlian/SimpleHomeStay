How to book?

STEP 1: From the calendar below, select only the night(s) of your stay.

Examples:
If for 1 night - check-in 1/1/2019 and check-out 2/1/2019, select only 1/1/2019.
If for 2 nights - check-in 1/1/2019 and check-out 3/1/2019, select only 1/1/2019 and 2/1/2019.

STEP 3: Check the TOTAL COST and press SEND.
